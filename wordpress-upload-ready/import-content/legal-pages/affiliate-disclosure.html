This website contains affiliate links, which means we may receive a commission if you click a link and purchase something that we have recommended. The price you pay will be the same whether you use the affiliate link or go directly to the vendor's website using a non-affiliate link.

We are participants in the following affiliate programs:

  • Amazon Associates Program (EU): We earn from qualifying purchases made through Amazon.co.uk, Amazon.de, and Amazon.fr

  • Various other pet product retailers and manufacturers

How We Choose Products to Recommend

We only recommend products that we:

  • Have personally used with our own French Bulldogs
  • Have been recommended by veterinarians or canine specialists
  • Have received consistently positive feedback from the Frenchie community

We NEVER recommend a product solely because of the commission we might receive. Your trust is more important to us than any commission.
