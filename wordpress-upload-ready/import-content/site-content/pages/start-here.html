Frequently Asked Questions

How long will it take to see improvement?

With the right approach, many Frenchie parents see initial improvement within 2-3 weeks. Complete resolution often takes 6-8 weeks of consistent management.

Can allergies be cured?

While allergies can't typically be "cured," they can be effectively managed. Most Frenchies with allergies live happy, comfortable lives with proper care.
