Emergency Situations

If you think your pet may have a medical emergency, call your veterinarian immediately. Never disregard professional veterinary advice or delay in seeking it because of something you have read on this website.

Signs of a medical emergency in French Bulldogs may include:

  • Difficulty breathing or excessive panting
  • Severe swelling, especially of the face or throat
  • Sudden collapse or loss of consciousness
  • Severe vomiting or diarrhea
  • Signs of extreme pain or distress
  • Seizures
  • Suspected poisoning
