üè• First Aid Steps for Allergic Reactions

For Mild to Moderate Reactions:

  • Remove from allergen source if known
  • Give cool (not cold) bath with oatmeal shampoo
  • Apply cool compress to affected areas
  • Administer antihistamine if prescribed: _____________ mg
  • Monitor breathing and behavior
  • Document symptoms with photos
  • Call vet if symptoms worsen

For Skin Reactions:

  • Rinse affected area with cool water
  • Pat dry gently (don't rub)
  • Apply prescribed topical: _______________________
  • Use e-collar if excessive scratching
  • Keep area clean and dry

For Digestive Reactions:

  • Withhold food for _____ hours (vet recommendation)
  • Provide small amounts of water frequently
  • Monitor for dehydration
  • Note frequency and appearance of vomit/diarrhea
  • Resume bland diet when approved
